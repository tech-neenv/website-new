## 1. Receivables Financing (Sales Invoice Discounting)

### Product Overview

Receivables Financing enables channel partners to unlock immediate liquidity from approved but unpaid sales invoices. Instead of waiting for buyers to complete their credit cycle, businesses can convert receivables into working capital, ensuring uninterrupted operations and growth.

This solution is ideal for distributors, dealers, and suppliers operating on credit terms with OEM corporates or large buyers.

### How It Works

1. **Digital Onboarding**
   Register and provide essential business and KYC details through our digital portal.

2. **Invoice Submission**
   Post credit evaluation, upload eligible sales invoices securely for financing.

3. **Fast Disbursement**
   Receive a significant portion of the invoice value directly into your bank account.

### Key Benefits from a Channel Finance Perspective

* **Accelerated Cash Conversion Cycle**
  Convert receivables into cash without waiting for buyer payment cycles.

* **Collateral-Free Liquidity**
  Financing is based on invoice strength and buyer credibility—no asset collateral required.

* **Fully Digital Experience**
  End-to-end tech-enabled process reduces paperwork, delays, and operational friction.

## 2. Payables Financing (Purchase Invoice Discounting)

### Product Overview

Payables Financing allows channel partners to finance purchase invoices and pay suppliers immediately, without stressing internal working capital. This ensures continuity in procurement, stronger supplier relationships, and improved bargaining power within the supply chain.

This solution is especially valuable for distributors and retailers managing high-volume or time-sensitive inventory purchases.

### How It Works

1. **Purchase Invoice Submission**
   Upload your approved purchase invoice on our platform.

2. **Supplier Payment**
   Upon approval, we directly disburse funds to your supplier.

3. **Deferred Repayment**
   Repay us on the agreed future due date, aligned with your cash inflows.

### Key Benefits from a Channel Finance Perspective

* **Stronger Supplier Relationships**
  Enable timely payments, unlock better pricing, and improve supply reliability.

* **Optimized Working Capital**
  Preserve internal cash while meeting procurement commitments.

* **Seamless Digital Financing**
  Simple, fast, and transparent digital process with minimal operational overhead.

## 3. Short-Term Working Capital Loan

### Product Overview

The Short-Term Working Capital Loan is a flexible financing solution designed to address immediate liquidity needs across the channel ecosystem. It supports day-to-day operations, seasonal demand, and short-term growth opportunities when invoice-based financing may not be sufficient.

### How It Works

1. **Application Submission**
   Apply digitally by submitting basic business and financial details.

2. **Tech-Enabled Credit Assessment**
   Receive quick approval through an automated credit evaluation process, followed by fund disbursement.

3. **Structured Repayment**
   Repay the loan through predefined instalments over the agreed tenure.

### Key Benefits from a Channel Finance Perspective

* **Rapid Access to Capital**
  Designed for speed to help businesses respond to immediate operational or market needs.

* **Collateral-Free Lending**
  No physical assets required, reducing barriers to access for channel partners.

* **Flexible Repayment Structure**
  Predictable instalments aligned with cash flow cycles and business needs.
